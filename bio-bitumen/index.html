The 4-Stage Process

Fromagriculturalwastetocertifiedroadbitumen.

A continuous, NHAI-spec pipeline - heat, refine, blend, certify.

01

Raw Material & Pelletization

Agro-waste - rice straw, groundnut shells, cotton stalk, bagasse - sourced within 50-100 km and pelletized.

02

Pyrolysis & Bio-Oil Extraction

Biomass heated to 450-550°C in oxygen-free reactors; bio-oil yield of 20-25%.

03

Refining & Blending

Oxidation at 230-250°C, then blended 15-30% with VG-30 per CSIR-CRRI specification.

04

Testing, Certification & Market

Penetration, softening, ductility and rutting tests; NHAI/MoRTH certification; supply to contractors, PWD & NHAI.
